About Jimmy Ardoin

He Walks Into the Fire When Everyone Else Walks Away.

Jimmy Ardoin is the owner and lead trial attorney at Jimmy Ardoin & Associates, PLLC, based in Houston, Texas. You call Jimmy when your business partner just stole your company, when an 18-wheeler changed your family's life forever, or when federal agents are at your door.

With over 20 years in the trenches and more than 50 jury trials across state and federal courts nationwide, Jimmy has built his career on one principle: show up and fight, no matter what.
